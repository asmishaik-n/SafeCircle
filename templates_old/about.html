🛡️ About SafeCircle

SafeCircle is a personal safety web application designed to help users quickly contact trusted people during emergencies.

✨ Features

  • 🔐 User Login & Registration
  • 📇 Trusted Contacts Management
  • 📩 Emergency SMS Alerts
  • 📍 Location Sharing
  • 🚨 SOS Emergency Support
  • 👤 Personal Profile Page
